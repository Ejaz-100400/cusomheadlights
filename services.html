HEADLIGHT CLEANING

Car headlight cleaning is an important task to ensure your headlights are functioning properly and to maintain the appearance of your vehicle. Dirt, grime, and other debris can accumulate on the surface of the headlights, reducing their brightness and potentially causing them to appear yellow and hazy. Keeping your car's headlights clean is essential for both safety and appearance. With clean and clear headlights, you can see the road better at night and your car will look more attractive. Regular cleaning is important because over time, grime, dirt and other substances can build up on the surface of the headlights, reducing their brightness and visibility. A little time and effort spent cleaning your headlights can make a big difference in your driving experience and the overall look of your vehicle.

PROJECTOR FITTING

Projector headlight fittings are a popular aftermarket upgrade for car owners who wants to improve their vehicle's lighting performance and style. Unlike traditional headlights, projector headlights have a more focused and intense beam of light, allowing for better visibility on the road and a more distinctive appearance. The installation process typically involves removing the factory headlights and replacing them with the projector headlights, which may require some modifications to the wiring and other components. It's important to choose high-quality projector headlights and have them installed by a professional to ensure proper function and safety. With projector headlight fittings, you'll enjoy a brighter, safer driving experience and a unique and modern look for your vehicle

FOG LAMPS

Fog lamps are supplementary lights installed in a vehicle to provide additional illumination in foggy or inclement weather conditions. Unlike regular headlights, fog lamps are positioned lower on the vehicle and are designed to shine at a wider angle to reduce glare and improve visibility in heavy fog. The lights are usually mounted in the front of the vehicle and can be switched on and off as needed. They are especially useful in situations where visibility is reduced and regular headlights are not effective. When using fog lamps, it's important to ensure that they are aimed correctly and to avoid blinding other drivers and to ensure optimal performance. Installing fog lamps can enhance your safety and visibility when driving in challenging weather conditions.

BAR LIGHTS

Bar lights, also known as light bars, are a popular aftermarket upgrade for off-road and on-road vehicles. They provide bright and efficient lighting that can be used for a variety of purposes, including off-road driving, work lighting, and emergency lighting. Light bars are available in different lengths, shapes, and configurations, and can be mounted on the roof, grille, or bumper of a vehicle. They typically consist of a row of LED lights, which are energy-efficient and provide a bright, intense light that can reach further than traditional headlights. Whether you're using them for off-road adventures, work, or to make your vehicle more visible on the road, bar lights are a versatile and useful addition to any vehicle.

HEADLIGHT WIRING

Headlight wiring refers to the electrical connections and wires that connect a vehicle's headlights to the battery and other electrical components. These wires provide power to the headlights and allow you to control them, turning them on and off as needed. The headlight wiring system is an important part of a vehicle's electrical system and is responsible for delivering power to the headlights, allowing you to see while driving in low-light conditions. Headlight wiring is typically comprised of several components, including a relay, switch, and fuses, which work together to provide reliable and efficient power to the headlights. Properly maintaining and troubleshooting the headlight wiring system is essential for ensuring the optimal performance and longevity of a vehicle's headlights.

DRL MODIFICATION

DRL, or Daytime Running Lights, are a safety feature in vehicles that are designed to increase visibility and make it easier for other drivers to see your vehicle during the day. DRL modification involves customizing or upgrading the vehicle's existing DRL system, or installing an aftermarket DRL system, to enhance the look and function of the vehicle. This can include adding LED lights, changing the color of the lights, or adjusting the brightness and intensity of the lights. Modifying the DRL system can give your vehicle a unique and personalized look, and also improve its visibility on the road. However, it's important to ensure that any modifications to the DRL system comply with local regulations and are safe for use on the road. Improper modification of the DRL system can lead to decreased visibility and potentially unsafe driving conditions.
